# Expense Reimbursement Policy

## Submission deadlines

All expenses must be submitted in the expense management system within **30 days** of the expense being incurred. Expenses older than 60 days **will not be reimbursed** under any circumstance. Expenses submitted 31–60 days late require manager + Finance approval” or “31–60 days are reimbursed only by exception.

## Travel

### Flights
- **Domestic:** Economy class only.
- **International (flight time > 6 hours):** Premium economy is permitted.
- **International (flight time > 10 hours):** Business class is permitted.

Booking must be done through the Acme Corp travel portal. Direct bookings outside the portal are not reimbursed.

### Hotels

Hotel rates are capped by city tier:

| Tier | Cities | Cap (USD/night, pre-tax) |
|---|---|---|
| Tier 1 | NYC, SF, London, Tokyo, Singapore | $350 |
| Tier 2 | Boston, Seattle, Berlin, Sydney | $250 |
| Tier 3 | All other locations | $180 |

Costs above the cap require **pre-approval in writing** from the employee's manager and a Director or higher.

### Ground transportation

Standard ride-share (UberX, Lyft Standard) is reimbursed without a cap. Premium tiers (UberBlack, Uber Lux) are not reimbursed. Public transit and rental cars (mid-size or smaller) are reimbursed in full.

## Meals during travel

Per-diem caps for meals on travel days, inclusive of tax and tip:

- Breakfast: $20
- Lunch: $30
- Dinner: $60

Alcohol is reimbursable up to **$25 per day** when consumed with a meal and only in the context of customer entertainment or team dinners.

## Equipment & software

See `IT Equipment Policy` for hardware purchases. Software purchases under $200 may be expensed directly; anything above must go through the IT procurement queue.

## Receipts

A receipt is required for any individual expense **over $25 USD**. Below $25 a receipt is not required but the expense must still be itemized.
