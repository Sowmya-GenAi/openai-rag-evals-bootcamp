# Acme Corp Employee Handbook — Overview

Welcome to Acme Corp. This handbook is the authoritative source for all internal policies covering HR, IT, security, and benefits. It supersedes any prior version and is reviewed every six months by the People Operations team.

## How this handbook is organized

- **HR policies** — leave, remote work, expense reimbursement, performance reviews, code of conduct.
- **IT policies** — equipment, VPN and network access.
- **Security policies** — passwords and MFA, data classification, incident response.
- **Benefits** — health insurance and related programs.

## Authority and exceptions

The handbook is binding for all full-time employees, part-time employees, and long-term contractors. Short-term contractors (engagements under 30 days) are covered only by the security and code-of-conduct sections.

Exceptions to any policy require written approval from a Vice President or higher. Verbal approvals are not valid. All exception requests must be filed in the People Operations ticketing system within 5 business days of the verbal request.

## Reporting issues with this handbook

If you believe a policy is outdated, ambiguous, or contradicts another policy, file a ticket in the **People Operations** queue with the tag `handbook-feedback`. Do not contact your manager directly for handbook corrections — the People Operations team is the system of record.
