# Health Insurance and Wellness Benefits

## Eligibility

All full-time employees and their dependents (spouse / domestic partner, children up to age 26) are eligible for Acme Corp health insurance starting on the **first day of employment**. There is no waiting period.

## Plan options

Acme Corp offers three medical plan tiers through Globex Insurance:

| Plan | Employee monthly cost | Family monthly cost | Network |
|---|---|---|---|
| Bronze | $0 | $200 | In-network only |
| Silver | $40 | $350 | In + out-of-network |
| Gold | $120 | $600 | In + out-of-network, lower deductibles |

All plans include:

- Annual physical: 100% covered, in-network.
- Mental health: Up to 24 sessions per year covered at 100% with in-network providers.
- Telehealth: $0 copay on all plans.

## Dental and vision

Dental and vision are bundled and **fully employer-paid** for the employee. Spouse/family coverage is $25/month combined.

## Wellness stipend

Each employee receives **$1,200 per calendar year** (paid quarterly, $300/quarter) as a wellness stipend. Eligible expenses:

- Gym memberships, fitness classes, home fitness equipment.
- Meditation and therapy apps.
- Wearable fitness devices.
- Standing desks, ergonomic equipment **not** otherwise covered by the home office stipend.
- Massage therapy and physiotherapy.

The wellness stipend is **separate** from the home office stipend (see Remote Work Policy) and the two cannot be combined for a single purchase.

## Mental health

In addition to the 24 covered sessions per year via the medical plan, Acme Corp offers an **Employee Assistance Program (EAP)** with 8 free confidential counseling sessions per issue, per year, with no impact to medical insurance.

## Enrollment and changes

Enroll within **30 days** of your start date. After that, you may only change plans during the annual open enrollment window (November 1–15) or within 30 days of a qualifying life event (marriage, birth/adoption, loss of other coverage).
