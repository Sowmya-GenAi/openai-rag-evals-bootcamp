# IT Equipment Policy

## Standard loadout

Every new hire receives a **standard equipment loadout** on their first day:

- Laptop (MacBook Pro 14" or Dell XPS 15, depending on role)
- One 27" external monitor
- USB-C dock
- Wireless keyboard and mouse
- Noise-cancelling headset

Engineers and designers may request a **second monitor** during onboarding at no additional approval. Other roles require manager approval for a second monitor.

## Refresh cycle

Laptops are refreshed every **3 years**. Monitors and peripherals are refreshed on failure only — there is no fixed cycle. To request an early refresh due to performance issues, file an IT ticket with diagnostics attached. Early refreshes require Director approval.

## Loss, damage, theft

Lost or stolen equipment must be reported to IT **and** Security within **24 hours** by filing an `incident-equipment-loss` ticket. A police report is required for stolen equipment within 72 hours.

The first incident in any 24-month rolling window is fully covered by Acme Corp. Subsequent incidents may be partially charged to the employee at the discretion of the IT Director, up to a maximum of **$500 per incident**.

## Personal use

Acme Corp equipment may be used for incidental personal purposes (e.g., browsing news, online shopping during breaks). Storing **personal media files**, installing **unapproved software**, or using the device for **another employer's work** is prohibited.

## Returning equipment on departure

All equipment must be returned within **5 business days** of the employment end date. Acme Corp provides a pre-paid shipping label. Equipment not returned within 30 days will be billed at the depreciated replacement value, withheld from the final paycheck where legally permitted.

## Approved peripherals catalog

The full list of approved keyboards, mice, headsets, and webcams is maintained in the IT portal under **"Approved Peripherals"**. Purchasing a peripheral outside this catalog requires IT approval before reimbursement.
