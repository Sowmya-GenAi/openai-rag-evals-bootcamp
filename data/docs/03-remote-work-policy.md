# Remote Work Policy

## Eligibility

All employees who have completed their **90-day probation** are eligible for hybrid work. Fully remote work (zero in-office days) requires VP approval and is reviewed annually.

## Hybrid schedule

The default hybrid schedule is **3 in-office days per week**. Tuesdays and Thursdays are mandatory in-office "core days" for all hybrid employees. The third in-office day is chosen by the team lead.

## Working from a different country

Employees may work from a country other than their employment country for up to **30 calendar days per rolling 12-month period**, subject to:

1. Manager approval at least 14 days in advance.
2. Compliance with local tax and immigration laws — the employee is responsible for verifying.
3. Working hours that overlap their team's core hours by at least 4 hours per day.

Working from a non-employment country for **more than 30 days** requires conversion to a contractor or transfer arrangement and must be approved by HR and Legal at least 60 days in advance.

## Home office stipend

Acme Corp provides a one-time **$1,500 home office stipend** for new hires and a **$500 annual refresh stipend** thereafter. Eligible expenses include:

- Desk and chair
- Monitor (only one per stipend cycle; see also IT Equipment Policy)
- Keyboard, mouse, headset
- Webcam (only if not provided in the standard laptop loadout)
- Lighting
- Surge protector

Coffee, snacks, and decorative items are **not eligible**.

## Working hours and availability

Hybrid and remote employees must be available during their team's **core hours** (defined per team, typically a 4-hour overlap window). Outside core hours, employees may shift their schedule freely.

Cameras must be on during external customer-facing meetings unless otherwise agreed with the customer. Cameras are encouraged but not mandatory for internal meetings.
