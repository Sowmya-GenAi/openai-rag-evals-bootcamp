# Leave Policy

## Paid Time Off (PTO)

All full-time employees accrue **22 working days of PTO per calendar year**, accrued at 1.83 days per month. PTO begins accruing on the employee's first day. New hires may take PTO before it accrues, up to a maximum of 5 days, with manager approval.

Unused PTO at the end of the calendar year carries over up to **a maximum of 10 days**. Anything above 10 days is forfeited on December 31. PTO is paid out in full on termination of employment.

## Sick leave

Sick leave is **separate from PTO**. All full-time employees receive **10 paid sick days per calendar year**, granted in full on January 1 (no accrual). Sick leave does not carry over and is not paid out on termination.

For absences longer than 3 consecutive working days, a doctor's note is required. The note must be uploaded to the People Operations portal within 7 days of return.

## Parental leave

Acme Corp offers **16 weeks of fully paid parental leave** for primary caregivers and **6 weeks** for secondary caregivers. Parental leave applies to birth, adoption, and legal guardianship. It must be taken within 12 months of the qualifying event but does not need to be taken in a single block — it can be split into a maximum of three blocks of at least 2 weeks each.

## Bereavement leave

Up to **5 paid days** per bereavement event for immediate family (parents, siblings, spouse, children, grandparents). Up to **2 paid days** for extended family. No accrual cap.

## Requesting leave

All leave must be requested in the HR system at least:

- **5 business days** in advance for PTO of 1–3 days,
- **15 business days** in advance for PTO longer than 3 days,
- **No advance notice** is required for sick leave, but the manager must be notified by 9:00 AM local time on the first day of absence.
